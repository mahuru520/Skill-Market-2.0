## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, founders, and operators use this skill to select a focused marketing module and produce practical checklists, draft copy, campaign plans, CRO recommendations, SEO actions, analytics tracking plans, and experiment outlines. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketing guidance could be applied as autonomous posting, ad campaign changes, tracking deployment, scraping, targeting, or email sending. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill as advisory documentation only; require explicit human review, legal/privacy checks, and platform-policy confirmation before taking external actions. <br>
Risk: Generated recommendations may include incorrect, misleading, or unsupported marketing claims when metrics, keyword data, or customer evidence are missing. <br>
Mitigation: Require source data for claims where possible and label assumptions before publishing copy, experiments, ads, SEO pages, or campaign plans. <br>
Risk: Analytics and targeting guidance may affect customer privacy or regulated data handling. <br>
Mitigation: Review tracking plans, customer list usage, and behavioral email rules with privacy and compliance owners before implementation. <br>
